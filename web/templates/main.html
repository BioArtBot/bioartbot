What is this?


The BioArtBot project lets you make art with biology. Click your mouse in the grid below to make your drawing and we will print it out with microbial ink - real living bacteria that produce vivid colors! We'll email a photo of your living work of art back to you. If you submit before 11:59PM EST on Oct 17 we'll also enter your work into ASM's annual Agar Art Contest on your behalf. We're ready!
